About

1. Cwiz is an online quiz event of Megalith IIT Kharagpur that challenges the nerds in the topics of Civil Engineering, Logical Reasoning and General Knowledge.

2. The participants are expected to participate in the leaderboard driven game for 5 days.

3. Top fifteen participants in the leaderboard at the end of the day shall get exciting goodies.

4. Top 3 winners of the cumulative leaderboard of all the days shall get Cash Prize at Megalith IIT Kharagpur 2019 during Closing Ceremony.

5. Cwiz runs everyday for 5 days from 3rd March 2019 to 7th March 2019 from 6:00PM to 11:59PM
